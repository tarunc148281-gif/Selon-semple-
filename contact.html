FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need to book in advance?

Walk-ins are always welcome, but we strongly recommend booking in advance — especially on weekends and during bridal season — to guarantee your preferred time and stylist.

How early should I arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 5–10 minutes before your scheduled appointment. This gives us time to consult with you and prepare everything before we begin.

How do I book a bridal consultation?

For bridal bookings, we recommend calling us directly or filling out the form above and selecting "Bridal Consultation" as your service. Our bridal coordinator will get in touch to discuss your full requirements and availability.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, debit and all major credit cards. No advance payment is required to secure your appointment — we only charge on the day of your service.

Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

Yes — please let us know at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or change your booking. For bridal appointments, please contact us at least 48 hours before.

Do you offer services for all hair types?

Absolutely. Our certified team is trained and experienced in working with all hair types, textures and lengths — from straight and wavy to coily and curly. We serve clients of all backgrounds and skin tones.
